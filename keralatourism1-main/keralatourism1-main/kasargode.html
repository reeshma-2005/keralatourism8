KASARGOD



Kasaragod is a municipal, town and district headquarters of Kasaragod district lies in the Kerala state. It is the well known destination place for the tourists in Kerala. It is situated on the bank of the sea with all the facility. Here you easily spend two or three days because there are lots of beautiful places like Ananthapura Lake Temple, Kasaragod beach, Kappil beach, Mogral river etc. These all the places simply takes your half day. The Ananthapura temple is totally enclosed by the lake. About 50 meters from the lake there is a pond in which crocodiles are nursed. These crocodiles are well known and trained by the devotees of the temple. Crocodiles never attack on anyone, they also eat only offers by the devotees of the temple. This show the kindness and strongest tradition of the temple and they believe in God. Another exciting place here is the beach and river where you can find the peace in the mid. These are the place where you can find the awesome scenario of the nature with enjoying the beautiful place. Bekal fort is also a very unbelievable place there. It is the historical spot with some of the area submerged in water.

KAPPIL BEACH

Kappil beach is a flawless tourist spot along the Malabar shoreline, located about 15 kilometres south of Kasaragod town.

LAKE TEMPLE

The only lake temple in Kerala, Ananthapura Lake temple is believed to be the original seat of Ananth Padmanabha Swami.

BAKEL FORT

Bakel is surrounded by a beautiful beach offering a mesmerizing view of the Arabian Sea from its observation tower where once the cannons were placed.

CHANDRAGIRI FORT

The Chandragiri fort is only 8 kms from Bekal and 7 kms from Kasargod. Regular bus services are available to the fort.

RANIPURAM HILLS

Ranipuram is a famous trekking trail in Kasaragod. Situated 750 m above sea level, this area was previously known as Madathumala.
